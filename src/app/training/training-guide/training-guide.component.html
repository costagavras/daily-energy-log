• The exercises in the app database are divided into Time-based, Quantity-based and Measured-output

  • Time-based exercises' energy spend depends on the duration of the exercise.

  • Quantity-based exercises' energy spend takes in account repetitions performed. Additional weight can be factored in.

  • Measured-output exercises' energy spend is supplied directly from the machine or tool used: exercise bike, elliptical, treadmill, pedometer etc.

  • Inserted exercises will appear in the table below input section. Changing the date allows to see, add or delete exercises in the past.

  • The log page displays past exercises grouped by date. It's possible to toggle the date detail, filter by name and show/hide columns.
